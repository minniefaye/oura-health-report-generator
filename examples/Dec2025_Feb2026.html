6) Medical Inference, Prediction, and Action Plan / 醫學推測、預測與行動建議

Priority 1 Moderate / 中

Sleep duration / 睡眠時數

Data Suggests / 資料推測： 47.8% nights are below 7h.

Medical Meaning / 醫學意義： Chronic short sleep is linked with higher cardiometabolic and mood-risk burden.

Action 2-4 Weeks / 行動建議： Set fixed sleep window; target >=7h on >=5 nights/week; avoid caffeine 8h before bedtime.

Track / 追蹤： Track weekly: nights >=7h, Sleep Score trend, daytime fatigue.

Priority 2 Moderate / 中

Activity pattern / 活動型態

Data Suggests / 資料推測： Mean steps 4994; low-step days (<5000): 52.2%.

Medical Meaning / 醫學意義： In preventive medicine, regular moderate activity improves cardiovascular and metabolic outcomes.

Action 2-4 Weeks / 行動建議： Build routine: >=150 min/week moderate activity; add short walking breaks every 60-90 min sedentary time.

Track / 追蹤： Track weekly active minutes + step consistency (variance week-to-week).

Priority 3 Low / 低

Night HR / 夜間心率

Data Suggests / 資料推測： Median 66.2 bpm; out-of-range nights: 0.0%.

Medical Meaning / 醫學意義： Rising night HR over several days can reflect physiological stress, poor recovery, illness, alcohol effect, or overreaching.

Action 2-4 Weeks / 行動建議： On days with elevated night HR + low readiness, reduce training intensity by 20-40% and prioritize recovery sleep.

Track / 追蹤： Use 7-day moving average of night HR, not single-day spikes.

Priority 4 Low / 低

Readiness + sleep score / 準備度與睡眠分數

Data Suggests / 資料推測： Sleep score mean 71.0, readiness mean 76.7.

Medical Meaning / 醫學意義： When both recovery indicators drop together, injury risk and overtraining risk increase.

Action 2-4 Weeks / 行動建議： Use a simple rule: if both scores are low for >=2 days, deload and prioritize sleep, hydration, and lower-intensity training.

Track / 追蹤： Track 2-day and 7-day trends; avoid decisions from one low day only.

Priority 5 Low / 低

SpO2 / 血氧

Data Suggests / 資料推測： Mean 96.21%; days <95%: 2.0%.

Medical Meaning / 醫學意義： Persistent low SpO2 patterns can warrant clinical review, especially if snoring, daytime sleepiness, or breathing symptoms exist.

Action 2-4 Weeks / 行動建議： If low readings cluster with symptoms, discuss sleep/breathing evaluation with a clinician.

Track / 追蹤： Track low-SpO2 clusters (>=3 low days in 2 weeks) and symptom diary.
